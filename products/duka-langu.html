Duka Langu

An application for shops, pharmacies, supermarkets, or any retail platform—point of sale, stock control, and operational workflows aligned to how you sell.

Best fit

Retail stores, pharmacies, and supermarkets that need faster checkout and stock clarity.

Delivery model

Configurable core product with tailored implementation based on your workflow.

What problem it solves

Retailers often juggle separate tools for checkout, inventory, and reporting. Duka Langu is designed to tighten visibility across the shop floor so teams spend less time reconciling and more time serving customers.

Key features

  • Retail and shop operations tuned for varied inventory types
  • Sales and purchasing flows aligned to day-end accountability
  • Reporting and performance insight for owners and branch leads
  • Configurable for pharmacies, supermarkets, and general retail

Who it is for

Independent shops, multi-branch outlets, pharmacies, and supermarket-style operators that need dependable software without unnecessary complexity—from small businesses to larger retail programs.
